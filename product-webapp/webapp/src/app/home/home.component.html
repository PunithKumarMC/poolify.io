Offering a carpool ride on Poolify is easy. To publish your ride, use our website. Indicate your departure and arrival points, the date and time of your departure, how many passengers you can take and the price per seat. You’ll also need to choose how you want to accept bookings (either automatically or manually), and you have the option of adding any important details you think your passengers should know about. Then tap ‘Publish ride’ and you’re done!
